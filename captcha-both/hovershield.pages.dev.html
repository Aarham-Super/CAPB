PROJECT SPOTLIGHT

HoverShield

Designed as a showcase for asynchronous widget loading, HoverShield stitches Google reCAPTCHA v3 and Cloudflare Turnstile into one resilient verification trigger. The Cloudflare Worker posts both tokens and surfaces a graded verification badge that is easy to audit.
